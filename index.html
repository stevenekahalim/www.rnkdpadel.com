FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How does the ranking system work?

We use an MMR (Matchmaking Rating) system similar to competitive games. Win against higher-ranked players to gain more points, lose against lower-ranked players to lose more. Your rank reflects your true skill level.

Is RNKD free to use?

Yes! RNKD is completely free for all players. Record unlimited matches, compete in all leagues, and access all features at no cost.

How do I verify match results?

When you record a match, your opponent receives a notification to verify the result. Both verified and unverified matches count, but verified matches carry more weight.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account at any time from the app settings. All your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.
