FAQ

Questions & answers

How does the ranking system work?

We use an MMR (Matchmaking Rating) system similar to competitive games. Win against higher-ranked players to gain more points. Your rank reflects your true skill level over time.

Is RNKD free to use?

Yes! RNKD is completely free. Record unlimited matches, compete in all leagues, and access every feature at no cost.

How do match verifications work?

When you record a match, your opponent gets a notification to verify. Both verified and unverified matches count, but verified matches carry more weight in rankings.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account anytime from app settings. All your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.
