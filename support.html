Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create or join a community?

To create a community, go to the Communities tab and tap "Create Community". To join an existing community, ask the community admin for an invite link.

How is my ranking calculated?

Rankings are calculated using an ELO-based system that takes into account match results, opponent strength, and score margins.

How do I log a match?

Tap the "+" button on the home screen, select your opponents, enter the score, and submit.

What if my opponent doesn't confirm the match?

Matches require confirmation from at least one opponent within 7 days.

Can I delete or edit a match?

Matches can be disputed within 48 hours of being confirmed. Contact your community admin if needed.

How do I change my profile photo or name?

Go to Settings > Edit Profile to update your profile photo and display name.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account from Settings > Delete Account.
